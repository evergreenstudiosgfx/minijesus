A Small Reminder That You Are Loved.

Keep it. Or pass it forward.

Welcome to the Mini Jesus movement. Whether you’ve found one or want to leave one behind for someone else, each Mini Jesus is meant to spread hope, faith, and a reminder that you are never alone. Imagine someone going through a tough moment… and right when they need it most, they find a Mini Jesus. A small reminder. A moment of peace. A sign that God hasn’t forgotten them. What seems small can make a big difference. This mission is personal to me. I’ve faced moments in my life where all I had left was faith… and I know what it feels like to need a sign that everything is going to be okay. If a small reminder like this can bring even a little hope to someone else, then it’s worth everything.
